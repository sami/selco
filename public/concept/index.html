From tape measure to trade counter

1. Measure up A tape measure and two minutes. The planner takes it from there, waste allowances and all.

2. Check the drawing The plan redraws live, so if something looks wrong on screen you've caught it before it went wrong in the garden.

3. Bring us the list Copy it from any planner and bring it to your branch, or order through Click & Deliver. The tick-off checklist covers the tools.

Quantities are estimates based on standard practice. Check coverage and your own site conditions before ordering, and have structural work checked by someone qualified.
