3 order lines · 6 checklist itemsTake this list to your local branch or Click & Deliver
  • Clean 20 mm gravel only. MOT Type 1 has fines that blind a drain within a season, and 10 mm packs too tight to let the water move.
  • Wrap the geotextile right over the top of the stone before capping, that is what keeps silt out.
  • No soakaway selected. Make sure the run actually falls to somewhere water can go.
  • Gravel finish doubles as a soakaway strip for surface water.
  • Quantities are estimates, verify product coverage and site conditions before ordering.
